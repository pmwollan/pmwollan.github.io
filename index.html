About

Take a look at the history of Aloha Fridays.

  • 2017-2018

    Our Humble Beginnings

    The idea branched from my father's company BERG located in Spokane, Washington. Every friday they have Aloha Friday where you wear an aloha shirt to work. Now it was time to make it into a lifestyle. Why just friday? Why not live every day like it is friday?

  • March 2018

    An Idea is Born

    Started out selling used Hawaiian shirts on EBay to start the idea. Got started on social media. Instagram, twitter and Facebook.

  • December 2018

    Transition to Blog

    The transition to having a good time and drinking with friends and trying to get others to adopt the lifestyle. This includes beer reviews, and inspirational quotes. Also the addition of the Aloha Fridays sticker and the creation of the logo.

  • 2019

    Phase Two Expansion

    Aloha Fridays is starting a Youtube channel with vlogs. Also starting to blog about the newest beer trends and favorites. The next idea is to create an Aloha Fridays playlist on spotify to listen to when you are sitting on that patio drinking your favorite beer. More to Come!
